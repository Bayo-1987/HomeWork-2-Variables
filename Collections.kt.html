//Definition of List and Array
A list and arrays are different ways of storing multiple values in a single location and are
generally stocked or restricted to one specific type.

We can have a list of integers, booleans, numbers or strings. Most times we don't mix the list
of integers or booleans or numbers together. Elements can not be added or removed

//Examples
var weekdays = listOf<String>("Monday", "Tuesday", "Wednesday", "Thursday", "Friday")
weekdays.get(0)
weekdays[0]

var myNetwork = arrayOf<string>("Mtn", "Zain", "Celtel", "Airtel", "9Mobile")
myNetwork.set(1, "Glo")
